
||Server refresh||
|F5|Refresh current server|
|Shift+F5|Refresh all servers in tab|

||Tree navigation||
|Left|Collapse branch or go to parent in compact mode|
|Right|Expand branch or go to first child in compact mode (if already expanded)|
|Up|Go to previous node|
|Down|Go to next node|
|+ (=plus)|Expand branch|
|- (=minus)|Collapse branch|
|. (=period) or * (=asterisk)|Expand all children|
|, (=comma)|Collapse all children|
|Space|Expand/collapse|
|Home|Go to server|
|End|Go to last expanded node in branch|

||Node context menu||
|Ctrl+S|Suspend|
|Ctrl+R|Resume|
|Ctrl+E|Execute|
|Ctrl+U|Rerun|
|e|Edit panel popup|
|i|Info panel popup|
|j|Job panel popup|
|m|Manual panel popup|
|o|Output panel popup|
|s|Script panel popup|
|t|Triggers panel popup|
|v|Variables panel popup|
|w|Why panel popup|
|z|Zombies panel popup|

||Search in text views||
|Ctrl+F|Search|
|F3|Go to next match|
|Shift+F3|Go to previous match|
|Alt+C|Toggle case sensitive|
|Alt+W|Toggle whole words|
|Alt+A|Toggle highlight all|

||Text views||
|Ctrl+L|Go to line number|
|Ctrl++ or Ctrl+WheelUp|Increase font size|
|Ctrl+- or Ctrl+WheelDown|Decrease font size|

||Trigger graph||
|Left|Expand graph via parent|
|+ (=plus)|Expand graph|
|- (=minus)|Collapse graph|
|Space|Expand/collapse graph|
